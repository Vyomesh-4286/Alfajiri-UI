Terms & Conditions

Terms of Payment

  • A 25% non-refundable deposit of the cost of accommodation is required to hold and confirm bookings
  • The remaining balance is due 60 days prior to the arrival date.

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellation should be in writing and will result in the loss of the deposit.
  • If cancellation occurs within 60-31days before arrival, 50% of the cost of accommodation will be charged.
  • If cancellation occurs within 30 days of the arrival date, 100% of the cost of accommodation will be charged.
  • Guests should have a travel insurance policy covering deposit paid to confirm reservation.

Insurance

  • Clients must take out comprehensive insurance cover before travel for cancellation, medical expense, personal baggage and money.

Rates

  • In the event of increase in any tax, training levy, or service charge, Alfajiri Villas, reserve the right to adjust all contract rates accordingly from the date of such change. Alfajiri Villas may at its discretion, but not less than one months notice in writing to the Operator, alter the contract.
